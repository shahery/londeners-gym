Standing dumbbell curl:


First stand holding a dumbbell in each hand with your arms hanging by your sides. Make sure elbows are close to your torso and your palms are facing forward direction. By keeping your arms static, Exhale as you curl the weights up to shoulder level while contracting your biceps.


Barbell shrug:


This is the best excercise for building muscle in your shoulders and upperback. First of all, Hold a barbell in an overhand grip with your hands positioned just outside your thighs. Raise your shoulders slightly straight up and hold the elevated position for three to four seconds, Then lower them under control.
